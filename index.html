ENERGYTE- Energy Technical Exchange is an events company headquartered in Dubai, UAE specializing in curating conferences, exhibitions, workshops, training and technical seminars designed for oil, gas, petrochemical, mining and water industries. We aim at conceptualizing and bringing your ideas to life. Our core values include accelerating knowledge growth, valuable technical exchange and building strong lasting relationships.
